The article discusses the increasing threat of cyberattacks on space missions and space-based systems due to the integration of technologies like AI. The U.S.-China competition in space has heightened these risks, with notable cyberattacks targeting critical space technologies in recent years. Private companies and government agencies are collaborating to enhance cybersecurity measures, including encryption and intrusion detection systems. Technology companies like Microsoft, Google, Amazon, and Nvidia are playing a key role in securing space technologies.
